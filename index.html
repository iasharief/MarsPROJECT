The Red Planet

  • Mars is 95% Carbon Dioxide, so we would die if we ran out of oxygen (depends how long we are there)
  • We need water, but the average temperature is -65º F, so it is either the water or us that will freeze.
  • Sunrises and Sunsets on Mars are very different, as they appear blue
